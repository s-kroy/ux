Profile Preview

I want to thank you for your interest in my work and profile. As creative professional, I get inspired by the world around us. I love to express that with inspiring designs with paint on canvas as well on digital surface. I do both UX and UI Design for interactive products, digital services and enterprise applications across multiple domains. In short, I build bridges between humans and machines as we digitize our world. I have more than 9+ years of digital design experience solving real world problems.

Story About My Journey

I was born and brought up at Agartala in Tripura. From childhood, I was so fascinated by painting and drawing that I used to sketch on school blackboard by chalks or by bamboo sticks on muddy playground. I always lived on the moon as a child and ... still I do.

Post School Education, I've completed Bachelor of Science from Maharaja Bir Bikram College. As I am passionate about art, hence I joined Govt. College of Art and Craft to study Fine Arts. Later I've graduate in Bachelor of Fine Arts. Simultaneously I've worked as child art teacher and durga puja festive-decoration projects as part-time job. Soon after moved to Bangalore with dream. Initially I faced great challenges. Slowly my preferences get changed and I restart my design journey.
