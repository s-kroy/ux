Profile Depiction

I’m a creative professional who loves to paint on canvas & touch-screen surfaces. I do both user experience design & user interface design for interactive products, digital services & enterprise solutions. I simply try to connect the dots between human vision with machine algorithms by applying design methodology and accessibility principles. I have 10+ years of diversified design experience across multiple domains for start-ups and leading corporate brands. I am always keen to learn new-age tech & soft skills to harness my problem-solving ability.

﹆ About My Journey

I was born and brought up at Agartala in Tripura. From childhood, I was so fascinated by painting and drawingthat I used to sketch on school blackboard by chalks or by bamboo sticks on muddy playground. I always lived on the moon as a child and ... still I do.

Post School Education, I've completed Bachelor of Science from Maharaja Bir Bikram College. As I am passionate about art, hence I joined Govt. College of Art and Craft to study Fine Arts. Later I've graduate in Bachelor of Fine Arts. Simultaneously I've worked as child art teacher and durga puja festive-decoration projects as part-time job. Soon after moved to Bangalore with dream. Initially I faced great challenges. Slowly my preferences get changed and I restart my design journey.

Design Expertise

Throughout my career, I have always been keen to learn new-age tech and enhance soft-skills to leverage my problem-solving ability which give me the opportunity to explore different territories of design. My optimal responsibilities range from planning design strategy to submitting design documentation in order to generate new revenue or re-engage with existing leads. Here are few UX practices I follow while crafting any designs.

Design Thinking

The creative ideation process that challenges usual assumptions rather than identifying alternative techniques.

Cognitive Empathy

Ability to understand another person's perspective. It involves being able to put yourself in someone else's shoes.

Ethnographic Research

A qualitative method of direct observation in order to capture behavioural activity in their natural environment.

Persona Analytics

A user persona is a fictional character that represents a specific type of audience for any products or services.

Information Architecture

The disciplined way of organizing, labeling & presenting information in a way that makes it easy to find & use.

Pragmatic Wireframes

A two-dimensional skeleton layout that demonstrates elements & features that will appear in the final output.

High-fidelity Prototype

Prototyping is dynamic replica of products or services used for analytical testing to improve design accuracy.

Interaction Design

The creation of physical or emotional dialogue between humans & digital environments or interactive systems.

Functional Usability

Usability means interplay of certain factors can be achieve key-specific goals with efficiency and satisfaction.

Hierarchical Typography

The art and technique of arranging type to make written language legible, readable & aesthetically appealing.

Crafted Iconography

The study of images that represent symbolic identification, artistic representation & interpretation of content.

Taxonomy Documentation

Taxonomy is the practice of classifying things according to type or category in order to create specific system.
