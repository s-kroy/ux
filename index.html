About My Journey

I was born and brought up at Agartala in Tripura. From childhood, I was so fascinated by painting and drawing. I used to sketch on school blackboard by chalk or muddy playground. I always lived on the moon as a child and ... still I do.

After school, I've completed Bachelor of Science from Maharaja Bir Bikram College. As I was very much passionate about art, so I joined Govt. College of Art and Craft for study Fine Art. Later I've graduated in Bachelor of Visual Arts. Simultaneously I work as child art teacher and also worked in festive-decoration (durga puja) projects. Soon after I moved to Bangalore with dream. Initially faced great challenges. Slowly things got change when I restart my creative design journey.
